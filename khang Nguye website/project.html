EGGSTENDER

Finished Praxis 3 Design Review

The design opportunity follows Shoresand Farms, a small-scale poultry farm in Lagos, Nigeria which is struggling with issues of food waste. An opportunity exists to preserve eggs in a sustainable and efficient way.

Frame Protection Box

Finished Praxis Showcase

Frame Protection Box is a transportation case for delicate space items. This design uses a framework to transfer the trauma forces so that no force will be directly impacted to the payload. This helps minimize the damage and ensure safe transportation of the payload.
