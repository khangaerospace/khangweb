About Me

Hi, I am Khang Nguyen,

I am currently a third-year student specializing in aerospace engineering at the University of Toronto. I learn and develop technologies at the forefront of hi-tech emerging markets in Toronto, Canada. My expertise lies in robotics and spacecraft engineering. I am passionate about science, technology and humanity. I am currently the ADCS Lead for the University of Toronto Aerospace Team developing the Attitude Control System for FINCH satellite. This website will be dedicated to my life, talents, skills, accomplishments. You can see my works, projects, professional careers and more.

If you wish to contact me click here. If you want to know more about me, please enjoy this website.

I will update this website regularly.
